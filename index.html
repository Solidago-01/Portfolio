Tales of Fort Denial

Desktop Controls: A: Z B: X Start/Game Menu: Enter Up, Down, Left, Right: WASD, or ↑ ↓ ← →

Mobile Controls: To play on mobile with touchscreen controls, use the following itch.io link: https://lgnadinger.itch.io/talesoffortdenial

Tales of Fort Denial is a handmade book written, designed, printed and bound by Marianne Dages, adapted here into an 8-bit visual novel adventure game by L Autumn Gnadinger. Learn more about Tales of Fort Denial.

Engine: GB Studio Sound Effects by Coffee 'Valen' Bat.
